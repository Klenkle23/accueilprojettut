Build Computer

With our PC configurator, you can easily choose from a wide range of components, including CPU, graphics card, memory, storage, case, cooling, and more. to build your new custom PC. We offer two different configurators to open the door of computing to more people, one being for the experienced and the other for the beginners.

Builder for beginner

You do not have experience in the field and you want to configure a custom computer, then this configurator is for you. Just answer a simple form without any technical words and get a custom-made computer that meets your needs.

Basic builder

You have experience in the field and you want to configure a custom computer, then this configurator is for you. Select yourself the components that suit you one by one to have a computer that suits you.
